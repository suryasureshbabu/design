Santa Claus in a Ferrari-engined Toyota 86 No reindeer-power needed for this Ferrari-powered sleigh for Santa Claus Donut Media, however, documented the entire process of fabricating and cutting and repainting Ryan Tuerck’s Ferrari-powered Toyota on their YouTube channel. The build began back in July this year and is now complete. They put it to test with some sweet drifts in a Christmas-themed video called Santa Sleighs – and it’s pretty mad. The Toyota 86 has been painted a festive red and leaves trails of dramatic white tyre smoke in its wake. There’s also string lights, flaming sparklers and red smoke spray to add to the Yuletide cheer in the video. Check it out.
